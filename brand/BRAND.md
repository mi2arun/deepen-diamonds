# Deepen Group — Brand Kit

Source: `deepen-group-logo.ai` (4 artboards). Exported PNGs in this folder.

## Logo
- Wordmark: **deepen** (lowercase, bold, rounded-geometric) + **Group** (italic).
- **The "p" contains a negative-space diamond** — this is the brand's signature mark.
  Use the diamond glyph on its own as the **app icon / favicon / kiosk splash**.
- Variants:
  - `deepen-logo-blue.png` — primary (blue on light) → light backgrounds, admin header, app top bar.
  - `deepen-logo-white.png` — reversed (white) → dark backgrounds, kiosk splash, deep-link OG card.
  - `deepen-logo-black.png` — mono → print, watermarks, low-color contexts.

## Application to the product
- **Customer app:** white cards on `#F5F7FB`; brand blue for the Hold button and active
  filter chips; diamond glyph as app icon. Kiosk splash = white logo on brand-blue field.
- **Admin panel:** blue logo in the sidebar/header; blue primary actions; keep data-dense
  tables neutral (ink on white) so the blue reads as "action," not decoration.
- **Deep-link share card (OG image):** white logo + diamond on a brand-blue gradient.
